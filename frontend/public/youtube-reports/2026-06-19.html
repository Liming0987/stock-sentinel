For valuation, Parkev uses two methods. His final verdict is a clear buy: the stock is meaningfully undervalued on DCF and at least fairly valued on multiples, with intact long-term growth drivers (international expansion, shelf space gains, margin recovery as acquisition costs fade) providing multi-year runway.
